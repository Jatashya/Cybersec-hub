# Dual Layer Authentication (2FA)

**Category:** Access Control

**Read time:** 3 mins

**Description:** Explore the difference between SMS 2FA, TOTP authentication apps, and hardware security keys to establish secondary defense boundaries.

![2FA placeholder](images/2fa.png)

## Content

Not all second factors are equal. SMS-based 2FA is susceptible to SIM-swapping and interception; TOTP apps (e.g., Authenticator, Authy) are stronger but still phishable if users paste codes into malicious sites. Hardware security keys (FIDO2/WebAuthn) provide the highest assurance by requiring a physical device and resisting remote phishing. Enforce backup codes safely and avoid providing codes via phone or email to unknown callers.
